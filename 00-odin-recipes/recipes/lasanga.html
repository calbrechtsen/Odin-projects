The Perfect Lasanga

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian pasta dish composed of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich meat or vegetable ragù, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and savory cheese such as mozzarella or Parmesan.

Baked to perfection, it offers a harmonious balance of textures and flavors, making it a hallmark of traditional Italian cuisine and a symbol of comforting, refined home-style cooking.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    • Cook ground beef (or a mix of meats) with onions and garlic, then add tomato sauce, tomato paste, herbs (like basil and oregano), salt, and pepper. Simmer until thickened.
  2. Cook the pasta:
    • Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and lay them flat to prevent sticking.
  3. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese with an egg, salt, pepper, and optionally some chopped parsley for flavor.
