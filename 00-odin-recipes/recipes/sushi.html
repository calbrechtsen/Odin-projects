Sushi

Description

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish featuring vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as fresh seafood, vegetables, and sometimes egg or tropical fruits. It is artfully prepared and often served with accompaniments like soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Renowned for its balance of flavor, texture, and visual presentation, sushi exemplifies the Japanese culinary philosophy of harmony, precision, and respect for high-quality ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice (shari)
  • Fresh fish or seafood

Steps

  1. Prepare the sushi rice:
    • Cook short-grain rice, then season it with a mixture of rice vinegar, sugar, and salt. Allow it to cool slightly before use.
