Tacos

Description

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish consisting of small, soft or crispy tortillas filled with a variety of ingredients such as seasoned meats, beans, vegetables, and fresh garnishes.

Typically topped with salsa, cheese, cilantro, and lime, tacos are celebrated for their vibrant flavors, versatility, and cultural significance as both street food and a staple of Mexican cuisine.
